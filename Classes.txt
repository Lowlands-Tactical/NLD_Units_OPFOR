@NLD_units_OPFOR
______________________________________________________________

The units are generally the same bit differ in terms of uniform.

The regular infantry come with the following cammo-types:
	WLD		Woodland
	DST		Desert
	WTR		Winter
	NFPT	NFP-Tan
	NFPG	NFP-Green

The special forces come in two types of cammo:
	SF		Black
	MTP		MTP

So you'll see this return in the units
